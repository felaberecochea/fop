Fascination of Plants Day is celebrated worldwide biannually on the 18th of May. We take this day to share our research and our excitement for plants. At Utrecht University we began celebrating this day on 2024, and we continue to do so on 2026.

This website brings together both editions of the exhibition. Here you will find posters, projects, and contributions from researchers working across plant science. Explore the exhibitions below.
